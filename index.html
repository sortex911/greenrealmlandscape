Green Realm Landscape

Green Realm Landscape in Thrissur, Kochi, and Bangalore is one of Kerala’s foremost landscaping and maintenance companies.

Formed through the merger of Garden City Gardens, founded by Mr. Sabu Mathew in 2009, and Green Realm Landscape, founded by Ar. Sibin M. Sabu in 2015, the companies have been working collaboratively since 2017 onwards.

The firm is driven by client satisfaction and quality service, supported by a team of experienced, young, energetic, and passionate professionals. The team consists of Landscape Architects, Landscape Engineers, Botanists, and skilled landscape workers.

For the past 15 years, the company has maintained a strong commitment to excellence, integrity, and service.

Our projects include landscapes for residences, resorts, children’s parks, schools, and hospitality spaces. Our services cover Design, Construction, and Maintenance of Landscape, Hardscape, Mixed Farms, Horticulture Consultancy, Irrigation Hydraulic Design, and Installations.

Services

Landscape Consultation

Professional landscape consultation by expert architects and horticulturists, offering expert guidance to transform your outdoor spaces with sustainable and aesthetic landscaping solutions.

Terrace Garden

Designing and creating lush terrace gardens that maximize green space, enhance air quality, and bring nature closer to urban living.

Butterfly Garden

Creating vibrant butterfly gardens that attract and nurture pollinators by incorporating native plants, nectar sources, and natural habitats, enhancing biodiversity and ecological balance.

Miyawaki Forest

Developing dense, fast-growing Miyawaki forests using native plant species to restore biodiversity, improve air quality, and create a self-sustaining green ecosystem.

Irrigation Design

Designing and implementing efficient irrigation systems that optimize water usage, support plant health, and ensure sustainable, low-maintenance landscapes.

Landscape Maintenance

Providing expert landscape maintenance services to ensure healthy, thriving, and well-manicured outdoor spaces through regular care, pruning, and sustainable practices.

Our Creative Minds

Er. Sabu Mathew

Landscape Engineer, Thrissur

He has over 30 years of experience in the landscape field, including 20 years as a Landscaping Engineer and Consultant with multinational companies such as Saudi ARAMCO, WESCO (England), and KEO International Consultants (UAE). He founded the firm in 2009, and for the past 14 years it has maintained a strong commitment to quality, integrity, and excellence, delivering projects across residences, resorts, parks, institutions, gardens, Miyawaki, and government sectors.

Seema K Sabu

Horticulturist, Thrissur

She graduated in 1986 with a degree in Horticulture from Thrissur. With a deep interest in plant studies, her extensive knowledge and experience are a true asset to our firm. She is the key decision-maker for all plant selection and horticultural aspects of our projects. She has collaborated on projects of various scales and plays a major role in site supervision and ongoing project maintenance, ensuring long-term health and quality of the landscape.

Ar. Sibin M. Sabu

Principal Architect, Kochi

Completed Architecture graduate specialized in Landscape Architecture, with hands-on experience across diverse landscape projects and scales. He has worked under the guidance of renowned architect Ar. B. V. Doshi (Sangath, Ahmedabad) and respected architects in Kerala. His project experience spans residential landscapes, resorts, parks, schools, herbal gardens, butterfly gardens, edible landscapes, Miyawaki forests, design sensibility, technical expertise, and adaptability across contexts.
